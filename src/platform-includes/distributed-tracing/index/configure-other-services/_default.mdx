Remember that in order to propagate trace information through your distributed system, all system components (apps and services) should have their own Sentry SDK configured.

#### Add a Sentry SDK in Your Other Services

To pick Sentry's trace headers up and further propagate them, you have to have the Sentry SDK configured in all your services. Take a look at the [respective SDK documentation](/platform-redirect/?next=/distributed-tracing/) to learn how distributed tracing can be enabled for each platform.
